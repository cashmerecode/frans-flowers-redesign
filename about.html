Our story

From farmers’ market stand to neighborhood shop

Fran started arranging flowers at a weekend market with a simple idea: work with the seasons and let each stem shine. Word spread, and soon a tiny studio became a friendly neighborhood flower shop.

Today we still order fresh every morning, compost our clippings, and wrap bouquets in recyclable papers. If a flower isn’t at its peak, we swap it — quality first, always.

What makes us different

  • Seasonal first: we design around what’s beautiful this week.
  • Made by hand: no assembly lines — just detail-minded florists.
  • Local delivery: same-day in our service area with careful transport.
  • Honest substitutions: same style and value if a stem runs out.
