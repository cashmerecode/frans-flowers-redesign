Delivery & FAQ

Answers to common questions.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes. Order before 2 pm for same-day local delivery. Orders after 2 pm arrive the next day.

What areas do you deliver to?

We serve the local metro area. Enter your address at checkout to confirm availability.

Will my bouquet match the photos?

We design with seasonal stems, so varieties may vary. The overall style and value are always matched.

How do I care for my flowers?

Trim stems at an angle, use clean water, add the provided food, and keep away from direct sun and heat.
